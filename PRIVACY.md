# Privacy Policy

> [!IMPORTANT]
> This document explains what data the **Design Tokens Export & Deploy** plugin collects, how it is stored, and your rights regarding your personal information.

## Data collected and why

| Data                                       | Purpose            | Storage                  |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------ | ------------------------ |
| Figma/Penpot user ID                       | Subscription check | Supabase (EU)            |
| Subscription status                        | Feature access     | Locally cached in plugin |
| Usage data (features, version, platform)   | Analytics          | PostHog (EU, no cookies) |
| Error reports (hashed user ID, error type) | Error tracking     | Sentry (EU)              |

Payments are handled by **Stripe**. Git tokens are stored locally on your machine only.

**Legal basis:** Contract performance (subscription) and legitimate interest (analytics, error tracking).

## Your rights

Contact figmacssvariables[at]yoriiis.com to access, delete, or export your data. We respond within 30 days.

## Subprocessors

Supabase, GitHub, PostHog, Sentry, Stripe, Netlify.
